Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our manufacturing services.

Our standard MOQ is 100 pieces per style/color, but we can sometimes accommodate smaller quantities for sample orders or new clients. MOQ may vary depending on fabric availability and design complexity.

Yes! We provide full OEM/ODM services. You can send us your sketches, tech packs, or just ideas, and our design team will create samples based on your requirements. We can also suggest trending designs that suit your target market.

After sample approval and fabric confirmation, standard production takes 25-35 days. Rush production is available for an additional fee. Larger orders may require more time, which we'll communicate upfront.

We implement strict QC at three stages: 1) Fabric inspection before cutting, 2) In-process quality checks during sewing, and 3) Final inspection before packaging. Our quality pass rate is consistently above 98%.

We work with all major shipping providers (DHL, FedEx, UPS) for samples and small orders. For bulk shipments, we arrange sea freight with competitive rates. We can ship FOB or handle door-to-door delivery based on your preference.
