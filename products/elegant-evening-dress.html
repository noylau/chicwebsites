How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor.
  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, the narrowest part of your waist.
  • Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waistline.
  • Length: Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the floor.

Custom sizing is available for bulk orders. Please contact us for more information.

Shipping Information

Shipping Methods

  • FOB (Free On Board): We deliver the goods to the designated port of shipment. Buyer assumes cost and risk once goods are loaded on the vessel.

  • EXW (Ex Works): Buyer assumes all transportation costs and risks from our factory.
